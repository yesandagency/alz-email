The Alzheimer's Association International Conference® 2022 (AAIC®), the largest and most influential international forum to advance dementia science, will be held July 31-August 4, 2022 in San Diego, USA and online. Submit your abstract today for the opportunity to share your theories and breakthroughs with the world’s leading basic scientists, clinical researchers, early career investigators, clinicians and the care research community.

You have two options for presenting your oral or poster presentation:

  • In person: Join us in San Diego to connect face-to-face with leaders and peers from across the field, discuss the latest research and network. Many sessions will be streamed to online attendees.

  • Online: Present your findings from wherever you are in the world.

Abstracts are due January 24, 2022. Submission options include:

  • AAIC poster, oral and featured research sessions based on the following themes:

    • - Basic Science and Pathogenesis

    • - Biomarkers

    • - Clinical Manifestations

    • - Dementia Care and Psychosocial Factors

    • - Dementia Care Practice

    • - Drug Development

    • - Public Health

  • Alzheimer's Imaging Consortium (AIC) preconference

  • Technology and Dementia preconference
