Now in its third year, the symposium will provide an overview of current needs, Alzheimer’s science, and practice knowledge to advance research informing the care of Latino/Hispanic individuals living with dementia and their families. Attend the Alzheimer’s Association International Conference® (AAIC)® 2022 to collaborate with researchers and health care professionals and attend mentoring and networking events.
